Pure purpose, perfect performance

CXC is built with a singular focus: to read the digital audio from your CDs with total accuracy. Every component is designed to perform with precision. The end result is pure, perfect listening. Every time.

Precision driving

CXC's disc drive—the renowned S3 Servo—spins CDs at the exact speed needed for flawless data retrieval. This precision engineering gives you the best possible audio from every disc in your collection.

Physical Perfection

The acoustically dampened metal chassis in CXC ensures nothing disrupts the digital signal. With coaxial and optical S/PDIF digital outputs, you can easily connect to any DAC or DAC-equipped amplifier, ensuring a cleaner experience, whichever way you listen.

REWARDING PERFORMANCE

CXC is for those wanting the pinnacle of CD performance. Its focused, digital-only design, premium components, and precise engineering make it the ultimate companion for high-end DACs and digital-first amplifiers, enriching any audio system.
