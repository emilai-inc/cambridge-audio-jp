Your journey into sound

Your first step into a richer listening experience, AXA25 will reveal details in your favourite tracks that lesser systems miss. Designed to faithfully amplify sound from any source, the AXA25 lets you experience your music with newfound clarity, depth, and warmth.

Setting the tone

Fine-tune your listening experience with independent bass and treble controls. Whether adjusting to suit your speakers, personal taste, or room acoustics, AXA25 gives you precision control over 25 watts per channel at your fingertips.

PURE SOUND, MADE SIMPLE

With its minimalist fascia, black steel cabinet, and floating base, AXA25 embodies Cambridge Audio's signature design. Timeless, durable, and built to last, it's part of an upgrade path that grows with your love for great sound.

Seamless connectivity

AXA25 integrates effortlessly into any hi-fi setup. With a 3.5mm aux input for quick plug-and-play listening and multiple RCA inputs, it connects to turntables, CD players, streamers, and more—giving you the flexibility to enjoy music your way.

Wired for Sound

Built for sound, AXA25 features hi-fi quality speaker binding posts for a secure, high-performance connection. AXA25's low-resistance terminals ensure optimal signal transfer, so nothing gets between you and your music.
